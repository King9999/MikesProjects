I made Trapper! for Game Prototype Challenge v17. The theme was "island" and "entanglement." From those themes, I made a game where the objective is to capture exotic creatures on different islands. Trapper! is a puzzle game; when you move, the creatures move in the opposite direction. The goal is to make the creatures fall into the pits and to avoid contact with both the creatures and your own traps. There are 10 levels to play. Each level was created by hand since I didn't think I'd have enough time to write a level editor. All levels are stored in an XML file.
