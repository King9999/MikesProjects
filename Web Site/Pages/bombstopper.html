This is a prototype Flash game. The objective is to stop a time bomb from exploding by typing random on-screen words correctly. My intention was to make a game that could help people to type, but at the same time make something entertaining. If you complete the level, you are rewarded with various medals (achievements) for meeting certain criteria. The words are pulled from an XML document containing words I got from a Scrabble dictionary. As ridiculous as some of the words may seem, apparently they are real words.
