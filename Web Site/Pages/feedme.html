Feed Me! is a game I made in 48 hours for Great Canadian Appathon 3. You control a small round creature that's constantly hungry, and must eat food to survive. Eat too much, and you only have a few seconds to burn the excess food before it's game over! The "!" icon is the marker that indicates how much you can safely eat. Your goal is to fill the meter to that point without going past it. As the level increases, the marker shrinks in size and repositions itself, increasing the challenge.

The source code is available for download. If you have an unlocked Windows Phone, you can compile and run the game.
